Kampung Sungai Sekawang

Kampung Sungai Sekawang is a peaceful fishing village in Negeri Sembilan, known for its scenic coastlines and revitalized mussel farms. It offers visitors an authentic taste of kampung life, blending natural charm with sustainable traditions.

Location

Kampung Sungai Sekawang offers a tranquil coastal escape from city life, nestled near the serene islet of Pulau Mengkudu and the quiet residential area of Taman Setia2. Its peaceful surroundings, framed by mangroves and gentle sea breezes, make it a perfect spot for relaxation, fishing, and reconnecting with nature.
